Adventure Tours

Deep Sea Diving

Surrounded by the Indian Ocean and filled with fresh water reservoir inland, Sri Lanka is the hidden paradise for extreme anglers. With a shoreline of 1,140 miles and a continental shelf of 10,000 square miles.

The seas around Sri Lanka is one of the most challenging marine game fishing locations while The outfalls of 103 major river basins and hundreds of estuaries, lagoons and coastal lakes all around the country are yet to be fully explored by sporting anglers.

Abseiling

Rappelling down a rockface whilst enjoying the stunning views of Ella rock and the Ravana waterfall will leave you in absolute awe. Flying Ravana Abseiling restrictions:

Minimum Weight: 30 kg
Maximum Weight: 90 kg
Age: 16 years and above Note
Please note that the abseiling slots are limited, hence walk-ins will be upon availability. Please call us and book ahead to confirm a slot.
For safety reasons, if you are pregnant, have a medical condition, or are under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or medicine, you are not allowed to participate in any activity.

ATV Riding

Hop on to a powerful four-wheeler and experience the ATV Discovery in Flying Ravana site in Ella, a spectacular destination for all sorts of adventure sports, another hip breakthrough by the amazing Flying Ravana Adventure crew, introducing ATV Sri Lanka. ATV Sri Lanka: 01 hours experience including training
Important Please note you’re required to hold a valid driving license for ATV rides
What is included Safety Gear: Full face helmets and boots Safety Lockers
Activity restrictions For safety reasons, if you are pregnant, have a medical condition, or are under the influence of alcohol, drugs or medicine, you are not allowed to participate in any activity.
Please call us and book ahead to confirm a slot.

Archery

Perfect stress buster that will get you relaxed and focused at the same time. Join us and experience the excitement of our outdoor archery range, a unique way to enjoy time with family and friends whilst learning a new skill along the way. Located within the premises of Flying Ravana, Ella, the archery range allows the visitors the opportunity to participate in all the activities and spectate.
What is included

Bow & Arrows (20 Shots per session)
Chestguard and arm guard
Safety Instructions & training
Instructor Supervision.

Whale Watching

The ever smiling, mischievous dolphins will put on a show while some of the oldest and the largest sea creatures, the humpback and the blue whales, will nonchalantly glide past you, when you embark on a whale/ dolphin watching excursion off the Southern, Eastern or the West coast of Sri Lanka. The ideal locations for whale watching would be Dondra Point (accessible from Galle, Hikkaduwa and Mirissa) and Trincomalee while the sea off Kalpitiya teems with an abundance of dolphins..
